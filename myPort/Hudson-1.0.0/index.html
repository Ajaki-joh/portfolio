About Me.

I am Adjanou Jac Joh, a passionate STEM educator and innovator committed to equipping the next generation with the tools to thrive in a technology-driven world. With expertise in Robotics, Programming, and 3D Modeling, I create engaging, project-based curricula designed to inspire creativity, problem-solving, and innovation in learners aged 6–18.

I lead initiatives that include teacher training workshops, student competitions, and real-world project development. My goal is to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, empowering both educators and students to embrace cutting-edge technologies like IoT, AI, and data handling.
